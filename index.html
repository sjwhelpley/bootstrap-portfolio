About Me

I am a junior Information Science major at the University of Pittsburgh passionate about web and app design and development. I love to collaborate with other to create awesome projects, using the variety of things I have learned to add my unique perspective to a team. Here are some more things about me:

Traveling

I love experiencing new places. In high school, I went on a musical tour of a few European countries and this past summer, I spent 6 weeks in Cape Town, South Africa learning about full-stack coding. I find immersing myself in new cultures is educational in many aspects.

Music

I have been involved with music for many years - about 9 years now, to be exact. I began on flute and continued throughout high school, performing in the marching band, concert band, and Cleveland Youth Wind Symphony. I now play piccolo in the Pitt Band and flute in the Pitt Symphonic Band. Music has added so many dimensions to my life tht I couldn't live without. Fun fact: my next goal is to get a keyboard and learn piano!

Design

My first experience with tech with in Web Design, and after going full circle around possibilities, technology and design is where I want to be. I have always needed a creative outlet in my life - I used to create a lot of art projects when I was younger, and I love expression through music. Now, I have found a happy medium between my creative and analytical sides.
