About me

So, it's been quite the adventure. When I started university, I was determined to go Computer Science and ignore the creative longing in my brain. But guess what? It ends up I just plain suck at technical interviews. And coming up with good code quickly. And I just don't really want to do software engineering or things along those lines. But guess what I do love? Building usable products, incorporating design, and working with others to create awesome stuff. Also, websites and mobile apps - all aspects and stages of them. So here I am, changing up my path and risking it for the hopes of reaching what I can see myself doing forever and ever.

I am a current junior at the University of Pittsburgh now planning on pursuing a degree in Information Science focusing on Human-Centered Computing, with a minor in Computer Science and a certificate in Digital Media.
